About Prayer Power Network

Raising a global army of prayer warriors, igniting faith, and transforming lives through the power of prayer and the Word.

Pastor Henry & Joy Onyirioha

Founders of Prayer Power Network

Pastor Henry is a prophetic voice, teacher, and prayer leader with a burning passion to see believers walk in supernatural power. He founded Prayer Power Network to equip the body of Christ for effective prayer and spiritual warfare. With decades of ministry experience, he has ministered across the UK, Africa, and online, seeing countless testimonies of healing, deliverance, and breakthrough.

Joy is a worship leader, intercessor, and women's ministry director. Her heart for prayer and prophetic worship has opened heavens in many gatherings. She leads the PPN worship team and oversees the women's prayer fellowship. Together with Pastor Henry, she has built a community where faith comes alive and lives are transformed by God's presence.

Our Mission

To ignite faith, build prayer warriors, and transform communities through the uncompromising word of God and the power of the Holy Spirit. We exist to see lives changed, families restored, and nations impacted for Christ.
