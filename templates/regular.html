Regular Events

Join us for our weekly, monthly, and annual gatherings. All times are UK time.

Weekly Sunday Charge

Sunday – 8:00 PM

Welcome to our Weekly Sunday Charge – a divine appointment designed to ignite your spirit, align your destiny, and position you for victorious living in Christ.

This is not just another service; it is a moment of spiritual awakening. Each gathering is a prophetic encounter where God’s Word comes with power, clarity, and direction. Here, burdens are lifted, yokes are broken, and lives are transformed through the ministry of the Word, prayer, and the presence of God.

Every Sunday, we engage in heartfelt worship, revelatory teaching, and targeted prayers that address real-life situations. Whether you are trusting God for restoration, seeking direction, or pressing into a deeper walk with Him, the Weekly Sunday Charge is a place where heaven meets earth on your behalf.

Join us this Sunday and experience the transforming power of God.

Midnight Battle Cry

First Friday of each month – 11:00 PM

This is a period of engaging the Night Season for Divine Interventions. Midnight is not just a time on the clock; it is a realm of spiritual transactions. Scripture reveals that while men slept, destinies were tampered with, but it is also at midnight that chains are broken, prisons are shaken, and God rises to defend His people.

Our Midnight Battle Cry is a prophetic prayer program designed to confront and overturn stubborn battles that resist daytime efforts. It is a call to rise in spiritual authority, to legislate in the spirit, and to enforce the victory secured in Christ. DON’T MISS THE NEXT SESSION.

Pray Into The New Month

Last Saturday of each month – 11:00 PM

It is a prophetic prayer gathering designed to position you ahead of time, so you don’t react to the month, you command it; for alignment, restoration, and fresh fire.

The Bible declares in Job 38:12, “Have you commanded the morning since your days began, and caused the dawn to know its place?” This program is a spiritual platform where destinies are realigned, atmospheres are shifted, and the voice of prayer establishes God’s agenda over your days.

In every new month lies possibilities, but only those who engage spiritually take delivery of what heaven has prepared. Through intense prayers, prophetic declarations, and strategic intercession, we will dismantle every negative carryover and enforce divine outcomes.

Join us as we pray, declare, and step into a month of undeniable testimonies.
